Biography

I am currently a master's student in Institute for Psychology, Chinese Academy of Sciences and a master's student at School of Computer Science and Technology, University of Chinese Academy of Sciences, under the supervision of Dr. Tingshao Zhu. I also received my Bachelor's degree from Capital Normal University in 2018.

My research interest includes computational cyber psychology and affective computing, especially for online/offline behavior analysis and psychological characteristics recognition.
