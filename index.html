Biography

I am currently a master student in Institute of Psychology, Chinese Academy of Sciences and at School of Computer Science and Technology, University of Chinese Academy of Sciences, under the supervision of Dr. Ting-Shao Zhu.

My research interest includes computational cyber psychology and affective computing, especially in online/offline behavior analysis and psychological characteristics recognition.
